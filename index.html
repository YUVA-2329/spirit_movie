About The Film

ONE BAD
HABIT

"Right from childhood, I have one bad habit..."

A brutal yet honest cop — a former IPS officer and academy topper — descends into the criminal underworld on a relentless mission to dismantle a powerful global mafia syndicate. Driven by inner demons and a haunted past, his quest for justice pushes him beyond every moral boundary known to man. Directed by the sensational Sandeep Reddy Vanga, Spirit is a high-voltage, larger-than-life action drama set to redefine Indian cinema.

Spirit Release Date LOCKED: Prabhas & Sandeep Reddy Vanga's Mega-Film to Hit Theatres March 5, 2027

Director Sandeep Reddy Vanga officially confirmed the worldwide release date for Spirit via his social media handles. The announcement was accompanied by a striking poster, with the date written boldly over it. The film, produced jointly by Bhushan Kumar's T-Series and Bhadrakali Pictures, is set to release in 9 languages including Telugu, Hindi, Tamil, Kannada, Malayalam, Mandarin, Japanese and Korean — making it a truly global cinematic event.
